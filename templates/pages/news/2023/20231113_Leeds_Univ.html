Presentation at the University of Leeds

DIC Explores Collaboration with the University of Leeds, UK

Our DIC team recently visited the University of Leeds to discuss potential collaborations on multiple research projects. The meeting delved into shared interests and explored avenues for partnership. Discussions were marked by a mutual commitment to innovation, sustainability, and leveraging technology for transformative solutions in sustainable materials.

The University of Leeds representatives expressed optimism about the possibilities that could emerge from the collaboration. This visit marks a pivotal step in DIC strategy to forge strategic alliances with leading academic institutions, creating a synergy that fosters innovation and addresses the evolving challenges in the sustainability landscape.

NATURE-DEMO Consortium Gears Up for Transformative Year Ahead

As we kickstart 2024, the NATURE-DEMO consortium, comprising top experts from diverse sectors, recently convened for a pivotal meeting. Partners came together in an online meeting to strategically plan the timeline for project contracting with the European Commission and outline the necessary steps to finalize the Grant Agreement. With shared enthusiasm and commitment, the consortium is poised for a year of impactful collaboration and progress in reshaping climate resilience across Europe.

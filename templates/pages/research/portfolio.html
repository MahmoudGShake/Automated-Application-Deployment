Sustainability and Circularity

The DIC understands the importance of sustainability and circularity in today's rapidly changing world. By creating frameworks that examine the direct and indirect effects of our industries, we help organizations to implement sustainable practices.

Related DIC Products:

Safe Sustainable by Design (SSbD)

Life Cycle Thinking and Sustainability by Design are key approaches to assess and improve sustainability and circularity performance. The DIC utilizes frameworks for SSbD that integrate safety, environmental, social, and economic considerations.

Environmental Impact

We evaluate natural resources, energy and water consumption, greenhouse gas emissions, and waste generated throughout the life cycle of a product or service. Using LCA tools, we quantify impacts and identify opportunities for improvement.

Economic Impact

We assess costs and benefits of sustainability and circularity initiatives through life cycle costing, cost-benefit analysis, and financial modelling.

Social Impact

We analyze impacts on workers, communities, and society, addressing human rights, labour standards, safety, and engagement using social impact assessment tools.

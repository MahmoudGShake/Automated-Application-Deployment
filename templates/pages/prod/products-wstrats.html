WStrats® is the first online decision support tool for waste management strategies, offering a wide range of multi-criteria assessment and optimization modules.

Highlights

  • Over 30 different integrated waste management strategies with material and energy recovery facilities.
  • Wide range of waste valorization processes, including incineration, anaerobic digestion, composting, and recycling.
  • Planning flexibility for 8 different waste streams with default and variable physical and biochemical characteristics.
  • Comprehensive calculations based on the most recent IPCC guidelines, World Bank databases, and the literature.
  • Multiple analyses covering the economics, greenhouse gas emissions, energy production, and land requirements.
  • An optimization mode to allocate the waste streams over various management facilities to achieve target goals.

WStrats® is a useful tool for different sectors seeking to develop sustainable waste management strategies.

Strategic Planning of Municipal Waste Management

Planners can utilize WStrats® to promote sustainable practices in their waste management strategies. The software can assess over 30 different waste management strategies, including recycling, composting, and landfilling, and can accommodate a wide range of waste streams with varying characteristics. By utilizing the software's comprehensive calculations, planners can evaluate the economics, greenhouse gas emissions, energy production, and land requirements associated with each strategy.

Waste Management in Private Developments

WStrats® can be used by private developers to design waste management strategies that prioritize resource recovery and sustainability. Using the software's comprehensive calculations, private developers can evaluate the economics, greenhouse gas emissions, energy production, and land requirements associated with each strategy. The optimization mode can also help private developers allocate waste streams over various management facilities to achieve target goals.

WStrats® is the first of its kind, providing a comprehensive platform for users to evaluate various waste management strategies and technologies based on a range of criteria.

Comprehensive strategies

WStrats® integrates multiple waste valorization processes and planning flexibility for different waste streams with variable physical and biochemical characteristics. The software utilizes comprehensive calculations based on the most recent IPCC guidelines, World Bank databases, and literature to provide users with accurate and reliable assessments of their waste management strategies.

Optimization modules

WStrats® incorporates novel algorithms for single-objective and multi-objective optimization, allowing users to select the best waste management strategies based on their specific objectives. The software's user-friendly interface and data visualizations make it easy for users to create and edit multiple waste management scenarios and analyze the results.

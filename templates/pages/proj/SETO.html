Congratulations to our Digitalisation Team for winning a Horizon Europe Project

The European Commission (EC) announced the results of the "Smart enforcement for resilient, sustainable and more efficient transport operations (RIA)" TOPIC ID: HORIZON-CL5-2022-D6-02-03, and DigInnoCent s.r.o. was proud to be part of the announcement!

The winning project, "Smart Enforcement of Transport Operations" (with the acronym SETO), aims atdeveloping a cutting-edge digital solution that enables authorities to access all information needed for smart enforcement of transport and safety regulations in real-time through the 'one-click' principle. SETO will leverage state-of-the-art information exchange technologies and expand the system to multimodal transport, such as port to inland waterway to road transport. DigInnoCent s.r.o. is leading a work package in SETO and is eagerly anticipating a successful collaboration with the consortium, which consists of 14 partners from seven countries, including Austria, Belgium, the Czech Republic, France, Italy, Ireland, and Serbia.
